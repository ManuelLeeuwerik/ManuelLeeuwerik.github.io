7.2 DVs

vaccine_standard = Please consider a situation in which a vaccine for COVID-19 is free and widely available. This vaccine was approved by the FDA in a standard authorization process, in other words, a process that took place on a typical timeline for vaccines. How likely would you be to take this vaccine? Reverse coded: 5 = Extremely likely 1 = Extremely unlikely

vaccine_fast = Please consider a situation in which a vaccine for COVID-19 is free and widely available. This vaccine was authorized by the FDA through an expedited authorization process, in other words, a process that took place on a faster timeline than is typical for vaccines. How likely would you be to take this vaccine? Reverse coded: 5 = Extremely likely 1 = Extremely unlikely

vaccine_diff = vaccine_standard - vaccine_fast

flu = Did you receive the flu vaccine for the 2019-2020 flu season (last fall/winter)?: 1 = yes, 0 = no
